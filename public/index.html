Welcome to the website of "The Hexagon Of Life"!

Are you a biology teacher? And do you have difficulties explaining evolution to your students? Then "The Hexagon Of Life" may be the solution for you!

"The Hexagon Of Life" is a program designed to help teachers and students with understanding evolution.
With this program, teachers can easily show signs of evolution, and let their students play with it for a while to see how evolution plays out.

Not only is the program easy to use, it is also free. So you can always download it and see what the program can do for you, what's to lose!

If you'd like to learn more about the program, go to our about or wiki page, but when you'd like to go in blind, go to the download page.
